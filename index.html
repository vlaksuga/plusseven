WE PLUS FORTUNE ON YOUR LIFE

We have been providing enterprises and institutions of the shipbuilding industry around the world with the Best Shipbuilding Products made in Korea for over 15 years. Through those years, we have been able to establish a rock solid, consistent and successful business by manufacturing & supplying the products with excellent product quality, prestigious customer service, and reasonable cost to our valued and appreciated clients. We boast that our success is due to our differentiated ways of work to make our customers 100% satisfied as below.

  • Engineering Capacity

    We can supervise engineering and technical aspects of products and their production process because we have our own 30 to 20 years experienced engineers specialized in equipment and machineries in shipbuilding industry.

  • Quality Assurance and Punctuality

    We monitor all process of production checking all the rules and specifications as well as the production lead time. Also we make sure that all the documents and test results are ready and correct before shipment.

  • Responsibility

    Our client’s risks are All Ours. We deal with all inquiries & requests from our clients with full responsibility by putting ourselves in our clients’ position.

  • After-Sales Support

    We take care of our products we have already supplied and consult our clients on them no matter how many years passed by since they have been supplied

We do not want to supply any products to our clients, but the best products in the aspects of both service and quality with our specialties above

The 100% customer satisfaction is our number one priority and we look forward to serving you.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you only supply with items above?

    No, we supply with almost all kinds of equipment, spare parts, machineries from bow to stern and inside and out as well as engineering supports. Whatever you want, we are confident that we can connect you with the most reliable manufacturers and we will supervise and take care of all the process of paperwork, production and shipment, based on our engineering capacity & various experience.

  • Do you provide drawings and consult in aspects of engineering & technical matters?

    Yes, we have our own engineers who have 20~30 year experience in the shipbuilding industry. We provide technical advice and drawings for approval based on your technical requirement.

  • What classifications do you deal with?

    We deal with all offices of classifications in Korea which are ABS, DNV/GL, LR,BV, RINA, NK, CCS and IRS

  • What countries do you usually deal with?

    We supply Ship Owners, Shipyards and agents of various countries such as USA, Greece, Japan, Norway, German, Sweden, Italy and more with mainly (not limited to) products made in Korea whose quality have been evaluated through our professional evaluation system.
